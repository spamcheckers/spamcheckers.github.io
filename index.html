FAQ

Common questions

Does it work without an internet connection? +
Yes. The entire blocklist ships inside the plugin ZIP file. No network request is ever made during validation — it's a pure server-side local lookup.
Which WordPress form plugins are supported? +
Out of the box: WooCommerce, Contact Form 7, WPForms, Gravity Forms, Ninja Forms, and default WordPress registration. More integrations added in updates.
How is the blocklist kept up to date? +
Updated blocklists ship with each plugin update. Personal licenses get 1 year of updates; Lifetime includes lifetime updates.
Can I whitelist certain domains for my clients? +
Absolutely. The admin panel provides whitelist (always allow) and blacklist (always block) that overrides the bundled list.
Is there a free trial or refund policy? +
Yes — 14-day free trial with no credit card required. All paid plans include a 30-day money-back guarantee.
